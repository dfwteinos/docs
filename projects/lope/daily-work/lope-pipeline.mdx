Lope is designed so recruiters can jump in, see what matters, and keep moving. Use this page as your checklist for onboarding yourself, preparing a search, and reduce admin work.

## First-time setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Accept your invite">
    Open the Lope invitation email, connect to your team's workspace, and start scouting .
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your calendar">
    As soon as you sign in with Google or Microsoft, your calendar gets automatically connected. Lope now keeps interviews, reschedules, and meeting links fresh automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import your roles">
    Create a new client or reuse an existing one, set the stages you care about, and pull in any candidates via ATS export, CSV, or the Chrome extension.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite collaborators">
    Share access with your whole team. Invite your colleagues and assign them to specific teamspaces and clients / jobs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sourcing from LinkedIn in seconds with _Talent Import_

- Open a LinkedIn profile and click the Lope extension.
- Pick the the client (and job, if you have several).
- Lope grabs the profile link, headline, location, and contact info, then enriches the record and structures the candidate's profile automatically.

<Note>
  You can also select multiple LinkedIn profiles and add them into Lope. The same enrichment will run - no need to select one-by-one.
</Note>

## Finding candidates with AI Scout

- Paste a job description or type a prompt (e.g., “Senior backend engineer, Python + Django, B2B SaaS, based in Berlin or remote EU”).
- Lope scans your imported candidates (from ATS, CSVs, CVs, LinkedIn) and returns a ranked list with a fit score.
- Find similar candidates based on a star candidate you believe is a great fit for the role.

## Using the Notetaker during your day

- **Focus on the conversation.** Run the interview as usual; Lope records, transcribes, and generates a structured summary so you don’t have to type while talking.
- **Review and share outcomes.** After the call, check the transcript and summary on the candidate sheet, add your verdict, and use it as the base for feedback to hiring managers or clients.

<Check>
  Lope Notetaker auto-joins all your interviews from your calendar - no extra setup or admin work needed!
</Check>

## Using Reports to present candidate fit

- **Generate role-specific reports.** Create AI-assisted candidate reports that explain how and why a candidate fits a specific role or job description.
- **Include all key candidate details.** Pull in core info like experience, skills, locations, links, and interview insights into one clean, shareable view.
- **Share with hiring managers and clients.** Use these reports as your “single source of truth” when you discuss shortlists, compare candidates, and make final decisions.
