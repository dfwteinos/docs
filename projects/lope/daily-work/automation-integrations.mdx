Lope quietly coordinates interviews, enrichment, candidates, and access control so your team always works with fresh information. Here is a plain-language tour of the most important systems and what they do for you.

## Lope + ATS: how they work together

Lope plugs into your ATS to keep data in sync automatically - no extra manual updates required.

- **ATS Export.** Export candidates from your ATS (e.g., Bullhorn, Greenhouse, Lever) as CSV and import them into Lope.
- **One-Way Integration.** For some ATSs, Lope can connect one-way to receive candidate data automatically.
- **Lope runs in the background.** Keep running your jobs and compliance in your ATS; use Lope to enrich, search, run interviews, and present structured shortlists to clients.
- **Updates.** When a candidate is hired, you can update your ATS as usual; Lope will automatically update it as well.

## Candidate sheet: your single source of truth

For every candidate, Lope keeps one central profile:

- **Core info.** Name, contact details, location, current role, links (LinkedIn, GitHub).
- **CV & Enrichment.** Parsed CV fields plus data pulled from LinkedIn/GitHub.
- **Interviews.** Candidate transcripts and AI summaries from the Notetaker.
- **Fit Signals.** Instant job fit signals in every candidate sheet.
- **Smart report.** Short AI-generated summary of fit against a specific role, which you can tweak before sharing.

## Data import & enrichment flows

- **CSV imports.** Upload candidate lists exported from your ATS or spreadsheets.
- **Bulk CV upload.** Drop a .zip of CVs; Lope creates candidate rows automatically.
- **Bulk enrichment.** Select a group of candidates and run LinkedIn/GitHub enrichment in bulk; track how many profiles were successfully enriched.

## Sourcing

- **Chrome extension.** Add LinkedIn profiles without leaving the page. The extension respects your Lope login so you never re-authenticate mid-flow.
