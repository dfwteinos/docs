## What’s new with the Lope brand

<Info>
Lope is simply the new name and look for the product you knew as Kaktus. Your workspaces, data, automations, and permissions remain exactly the same—just with a refreshed gradient and updated typography from the Lope brand book.
</Info>

## How to use this guide

- **Skim, don’t memorize.** Each page is a quick briefing you can read in under three minutes.
- **Stay in the product.** Every section references real buttons, panels, or automations you already see in Lope.
- **Share with clients.** Feel free to send the Daily Work page to hiring managers who want to understand your process.
