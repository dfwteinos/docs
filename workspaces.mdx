A **workspace** is the top-level container for your recruitment organization in Lope. Everything you do: teamspaces, clients, jobs, and candidates—lives within a workspace.

## How workspaces work

When you **sign up** for Lope, a workspace is automatically created for you. The default name is based on your profile (e.g., "Sarah's Workspace"). You can rename it at any time from the workspace settings.

### What a workspace contains

- **Team members** — People you invite to collaborate
- **Teamspaces** — Collaboration containers that organize your clients and jobs

### One workspace per organization

Each **user** belongs to one **workspace**. If you're a recruiting agency with multiple teams, or a freelance recruiter working with different clients, you organize everything inside that single workspace using teamspaces.

## Workspace membership

- **Admin** — The user who created the workspace (or the first user to complete signup) is the workspace admin.
- **Invited members** — You invite team members by email. When they accept, they join your workspace and gain access based on the teamspaces and clients they're added to.

### Inviting team members

<Frame>
  ![Invite Members](/images/InviteMembers.png)
</Frame>

Use the workspace dropdown (top of the sidebar) to invite new members. They'll receive an email with a secure link. Once they accept, they're added to your workspace.

**Tip:** New members are automatically added to all **public** teamspaces in your workspace. Private teamspaces require an explicit invite.

<Tip>
  **Tip:** New members are automatically added to all **public** teamspaces in your workspace. **Private** teaamspaces require an explicit invite from any of the members of that teamspace.
</Tip>

## Workspace settings

From the workspace dropdown, you can:

- **Rename** the workspace.
- **Change the workspace photo** (logo).
- **Invite team members.**
- **View or leave** the workspace (members can leave; creators can delete the workspace).

## Hierarchy overview

Your workspace sits at the top of Lope's structure:

```text
Workspace
└── Teamspaces
    └── Clients
        └── Jobs (if any)
            └── Candidates
```

Understanding this hierarchy helps you organize your recruitment workflow. See [Teamspaces](/concepts/teamspaces), [Clients](/concepts/clients), and [Jobs](/concepts/jobs) for details on each level.
